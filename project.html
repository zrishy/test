Introduction

osu! is a rhythm game primarily developed, published and created by Dean "peppy" Herbert. Originally released for Microsoft Windows on September 16, 2007, the game has also been ported to macOS. Its gameplay is based on titles including Osu! Tatakae! Ouendan, Taiko no Tatsujin, Beatmania IIDX, Elite Beat Agents, O2Jam, and DJMax. The game has an active e-sports community, and is also sometimes recommended by professional players of other games to practice or warm-up.

Interview with Billy Brown

Q How long have you been playing rhythm games ?

A 1.5 Years

Q What are some things that you practice regularly to improve?

A I practice accuracy, which is very important as being more accurate can boost other skills.

Q Has playing rhythm games taught you any valuable strategies that can be applied to other activites?

A Yes, I have learned a few strategies, and mindsets that can be applied to other activities. One is that you cannot force any specific day to be a good day or not, you just have to do the best you can with that days set of conditions. Also even if you think you will have a bad day you might suprise yourself!

Q Do you think that playing rhythm games can help everyone learn crucial ways to attack problems in everyday life?

A Definitely, I feel like I can approach many different scenarios in my environment of school and rock climbing, with strategies I have learned from playing various rhythm gaming!
